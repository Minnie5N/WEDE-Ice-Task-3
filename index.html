Welcome to Movie Mania

"One Of Them Days" is a 2025 American buddy comedy film directed by Lawrence Lamont and written by Syreeta Singleton. The film stars Keke Palmer and SZA (in her film debut) as two friends who must scrambke to find rent money after one of their boyfriends blows it. The film just tells the story of "trying to make it through the day," no matter how bad or unfortunate a circumstance may be.
