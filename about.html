About Us

We are Starr Studios, a team of storytellers passionate about creating films that capture the heart and soul of everyday life. Directed by Lawrence Lamont, “One of Them Days” brings together the unique talents of SZA and Keke Palmer. Our mission is to highlight authentic stories with powerful performances and unforgettable soundtracks.

Our Vision

At Starr Studios, we believe movies can do more than entertain — they can inspire change, spark conversations and shine a light on what it means to be human. With “One of Them Days,” our goal was to showcase the complexities of friendship and the challenges of pursuing dreams in a world that doesn’t always play fair.

Meet the Director

Lawrence Lamont, the director of “One of Them Days,” is an American filmmaker, director, screenwriter, and producer, born and bread in Detroit. With a background in both music and film, Lawrence's’s unique vision brings depth, rhythm and authenticity to the big screen.

Starring

SZA stars as Alyssa, a young woman who's quirky and carefree, almost like the wild card in your friend group.

Keke Palmer shines as Dreux, Alyssa’s best friend, who is determined to balance her professional ambitions with her personal life. Known for her energy and versatility, Keke brings both humor and depth to the role.
