Synopsis

In “One of Them Days,” best friends and roommates Dreux(Keke Palmer) and Alyssa (SZA) are about to have One Of Them Days. When they discover Alyssa’s boyfriend has blown their rent money, the duo finds themselves going to extremes in a comical race against the clock.
